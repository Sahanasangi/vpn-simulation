Understanding VPN Technology

A VPN (Virtual Private Network) encrypts your internet connection, hiding your online activity and protecting your data.

Step 1: Connect to a VPN Server

Your device connects to a remote VPN server through a secure tunnel.

Step 2: Encryption & IP Masking

All data is encrypted, and your real IP address is hidden.

Step 3: Secure & Anonymous Browsing

Now you can browse safely with full privacy!

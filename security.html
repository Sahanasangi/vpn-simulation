Why Use a VPN?

VPNs provide **strong security** and **privacy** for your online activities. Here’s how:

🔐 Encryption

Encrypts your data to protect it from hackers.

🌍 IP Masking

Hides your real IP address for full anonymity.

🚫 No-Log Policy

Your browsing history is never recorded.

⚡ Kill Switch

Automatically disconnects if VPN connection fails.
